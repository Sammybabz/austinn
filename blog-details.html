Top 3 Reasons To Redesign Your Webiste

4

SEP


Nowadays, it’s extremely important for any business or an Organization to have a perfect looking,intuitive, responsive website. Many people, while doing their research, check your company’s online activity along with your competitors’ before they call you or fill out a ”request a quote” form. Your organization website is an essential element that users base their first impression on. And, if done properly, it could be your 24/7 salesperson or could also be your active marketer. Many companies understand the importance of a user-friendly site and see the problems with their current one, but are still unsure about when to start the redesign process

A website redesign always entails a lot of work. It doesn’t matter if you do it yourself or hire a freelancer, IT Teams,or agency to do it for you. A redesign not only requires time but also an allocated budget. The best part, though, is the excitement of having a brand-new online presence at the end of the process that you will be proud to show everyone.

1. It Looks Outdated
Companies go through a redesign process for different reasons, but the main one is the old-looking site. You will not believe how many companies still have websites that look like they were done a long time ago. Even people or User with no experience in web design can tell they’re outdated. This could hurt the business, especially if users are choosing between several competitors. Consumers will most likely go with a company that has a nice-looking, easy-to-use or user friendly website because it provides fast use and also the assurance that the company is growing and on top of the latest trends and technologies

2. It is not responsive and does not provide a seamless experience across all devices.
It was so easy for companies to maintain their desktop-only sites several years ago when people didn’t use their mobile devices to shop, order food, do research and connect with their friends. These days users may be looking at your site from a Device just like, desktop, laptop, tablet or many variations of mobile devices. And they expect a perfect experience from all of these entry points. They want to make sure that if they saved the product in their cart on mobile, it is still there when they go to the site on their Device. They want to be able to do the research on their mobile Device and share the information they’ve found with friends and colleagues. And you may lose these people as customers if your site does not provide a smooth experience across all devices.

3. It has a bad user experience and is hard to navigate.
How often do you check what your competitors are doing? Once a year, once a month, daily? Unfortunately, many companies don’t pay enough attention to their competition until it’s too late of which the companies would have lost much out look due to their ignorance of not taking time to get update or plan on improving their web. The technology is progressing, and changing so fast with a total different outlook. Someone who is on top of the latest trends and understands users’ behavior and also their request could have the chance to move far ahead in the game. Always do your research and see what people in your industry are doing on their sites, check the new features they’ve implemented, see what the big guys in your industry are doing and implement that functionality to provide the best experience to your users and convert them into customers. When you create a new site for your company, always make sure to build it so that it can grow with your business and you can add additional features and functionalities without the need to redo the entire site.
