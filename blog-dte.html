Consumer behavior has changed drastically Today, the number of people who use the internet to find businesses has increased manifold and yellow pages have nearly been reduced to dust. In 2017, 97% of people searched online to find a local business near them and it keep increasing day by day.Furthermore, in the digital era there have been much improvement between the internet and it’s audience of which most companies or business make use of the advantage to drive or pull their audience to a closer range of which they share all latest and new features concerning their products and also they give update on everything which their customer might request in other to provide convenience and a better clarification on their goods to the audience......Top 5 Reason why every business need a website

A website redesign always entails a lot of work. It doesn’t matter if you do it yourself or hire a freelancer, IT Teams,or agency to do it for you. A redesign not only requires time but also an allocated budget. The best part, though, is the excitement of having a brand-new online presence at the end of the process that you will be proud to show everyone.

1. Increasing Company Credibility
If you want to show the world that you take your business seriously, then it is time to invest in a professional website. In an age when more than 50% of smartphone users discover a new company or product while searching on their smartphones, not having a website can damage your credibility. According to a study, 75% of online users accepted to judge a company’s credibility based on its website’s design. In the end, people are likely to engage with a company they can trust, and the website is the stepping stone towards building that relationship.

2. Increase In Daily Sales
getting a website for your business is an investment of which you will be having more audience drawn towards your product and more potential customer which will be more interested in what you offer to them all because In the digital transformation era, creating a website on free tools such as WordPress.com and Elementor don’t cost a lot of money, but provides more bang for your buck. A search-engine-optimized website lets you be in front of thousands of more potential consumers without spending much. It can help you reach a wider target audience for promoting your services or products. The content on the website also influences buying decisions and commercial transactions, no matter which industry you are in.

3. Working 6hrs Earning 24hrs
: wow you will feel like how is this ever going to be possible but the truth is it is 100% possible in reality. Having a website means that your content is available to users anytime during the day, at their convenience. So whether someone visits your website in the dead of the night or during the day, in some way you’re always interacting with them, and can say goodbye to that ‘closed for business’ sign. Being accessible at all times supports marketing and increases sales dramatically, and this is especially true for e-commerce companies.
